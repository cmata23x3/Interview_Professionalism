Problem Statement

Interviews are an envitable aspect of every college student's life and rarely do young students feel that they are prepared to do well.
But fear not, our website is here to help!

Motivation

As college students ourselves, we, the creators of Interviewing 101, feel that we have some knowledge and resources to pass on to posterity. Wishing that our mistakes are not doomed to be repeated, we have decided to design a website with some tips, tricks, and stories of the good, the bad, and the ugly of interviewing.

Overview

Interviewing is not just talking to a company employee and hoping for the best. It begins before the interview and continues past it. To help you understand this, the website will offer advice for interviewing in chronological order of events. We have compiled information from our own experiences, from the experiences of our colleagues at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and from the experiences of current interviewers at Intel, Microsoft, and LMI.
